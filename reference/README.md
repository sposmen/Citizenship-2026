# Reference — Official Study Guide

This folder holds the **official source of truth** for the quiz questions.

## Files

- **`discover-canada-2021-official.epub`** — *Discover Canada: The Rights and
  Responsibilities of Citizenship* (Ebook `Ci1-11/2021E-EPUB`,
  ISBN 978-0-660-39275-2). © Her Majesty the Queen in Right of Canada,
  represented by the Minister of Citizenship and Immigration Canada, 2021.
- **`discover-canada-2021-text.txt`** — Plain-text extraction of the EPUB,
  for quick searching / diffing against `../questions.js`.

The only official study guide for the Canadian citizenship test is *Discover
Canada*, available free from Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada
(IRCC). All questions in this app are based on facts stated in this guide.

## Corrections applied after cross-checking against the official guide

The question pool was originally transcribed from a third-party video. After
comparing it against this official guide, the following inconsistencies were
corrected:

1. **House of Commons / electoral districts → 308** (was 338).
   The guide states: *"Canada is divided into 308 electoral districts."*
   (338 is the current real-world number, but the official guide says 308, so
   the app matches the guide.)

2. **Major political parties → four** (was three).
   The guide states: *"There are four major political parties currently
   represented in the House of Commons: the Bloc Québécois, Conservative Party,
   Liberal Party and New Democratic Party."*

3. **Removed the "which is NOT a Canadian invention → electric light bulb"
   question.** The guide explicitly credits the electric light bulb to
   Canadians *Matthew Evans and Henry Woodward*, so the premise was false.
   Replaced with a valid question on the inventor of the snowmobile
   (Joseph-Armand Bombardier), which the guide states directly.

Time-sensitive facts (current Prime Minister, Governor General, etc.) are
intentionally **excluded** from the pool, since they change between editions
and are not reliable test material.
